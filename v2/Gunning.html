Gunning

Wild Style
The wild style is the traditional approach to randomly aiming pieces into the cannons. This is where you should start but most people end up choosing to progress to the two-cannon loop style because it's just so much easier at high speeds.

Two-cannon loop style
This style involves creating loops around two cannons at a time.

Board Sitting
This video just goes through what to look for when you need to board sit on a sloop.

Central Looping
This is at first looks a very easy gunning style which it is. However it requires extremely good reflexes in order to master but if you have those reflexes this is by far the fastest gunning style in my opinion. The loops are so much faster to set up and there is no board hopping!
