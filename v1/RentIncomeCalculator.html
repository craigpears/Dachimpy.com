About this calculator:

This is only an estimation assuming that 95% of all stalls are deluxe and this does not include any fort/palace trading
5.5k is deducted for fort/palace upkeep

Islands also recieve 10% of all sales tax when a product is ordered so this is only a value for part of an islands income.

Average stall baseline tax for rent is calculated as: ((Deluxe Rent /100) *95) + ( ((Medium Rent + Small Rent)/200)*5), counting how many different types of different stall sizes is a bit too much to do.

While this calculator does not show all of an islands income, the majority of most islands income will be from stall rent so it can provide a basis of comparison for different islands.
