If you are facing the wrong direction, then you can use a whirlpool to turn around. To do this simply enter the whirlpool, wait once and you will then find yourself facing the opposite direction.

 


The first thing that will happen once you enter a whirlpool is that you will go to the opposite corner and the ship will be rotated 90 degrees, therefore after you go forward you need to wait a turn. On the second turn you will return to the place that you entered the whirlpool and can then either go forwards, left or right depending on where you want to go to.
