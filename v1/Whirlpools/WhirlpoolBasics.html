Whirlpool Basics

Whirlpools are the thing that most people have trouble with so I have split it up into several sections.

Whirlpools basically just rotate you 90 degrees and move you to the other side of the whirlpool as you can see in the little animation above. It is VERY important that you completely understand whirlpools before you try to bnav, one of the biggest problems I see is that people spend half of their time trying to figure out how to move and how to use the whirlpools and not concentrating on how to actually hit the brigand/barbarian.

Whirlpool Effects:
1. Go to the opposite square
2. Rotate 90 Degrees
